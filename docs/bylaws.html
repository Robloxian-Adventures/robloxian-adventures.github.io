Robloxian Adventures Corporate Bylaws

 

Effective September 20th, 2021

 

The purpose of this document is to establish the corporate governance of Robloxian Adventures ​and/or ​its subsidiaries that are specified. Information on this document is deemed public and official by ​Robloxian Adventures ​and can be utilized as a reference in situations where it is deemed a necessity. This document and the information listed in the articles below are subjected to be changed without prior notice.

 

 

RA Bylaws

 

Article I: Leadership

 

1.1.            Owners​: ​Individuals holding ownership at Robloxian Adventures are considered to hold full responsibility of the group, its subsidiaries, and all associated assets. Ownership of Robloxian Adventures is stated below:

a. ​jody7777​- Owner, Holder of Mt. Everest

b. ​CooperGlassMD​- Owner, Chairmen of the Board

c. AristotleCreed- Co-Owner, Chief Executive Officer

 

1.2.            Officers​​ of Robloxian Adventures​:​ Officers of the Robloxian Adventures are classified as individuals who hold a senior management rank within the corporation and include individuals who hold ownership. Senior management ranks, as of ​May 21st, 2020​, include individuals who are ranked Deputy Director ​and above.

 

1.3.            Board of Directors​: ​The Board of Directors are individuals who are responsible for an essential component of the operations at Robloxian Adventures. A Board’s mandate is to establish policies for corporate management and make decisions on major company issues. In addition to those duties,   directors are responsible for helping a corporation set broad goals, supporting executive duties, and ensuring the company has adequate, well-managed resources at its disposal. Prospective directors must be implemented through a referral from a COO and approved by either the Chief Executive Officer or the Chairmen. It must be

 

understood that prospective directors do not have to have prior work experience in Robloxian Adventures but those with experience in Robloxian Adventures will be looked at first. Prospective directors adhere to the responsibilities associated with their position and are distinguished individuals who have long term experiences in a previous leadership role from another group. As of November 10th, 2020​, the maximum number of members allowed on the Board is ​11​.

 

1.4.            Hierarchy​: ​The Robloxian Adventures and its subsidiaries utilize a structural system of hierarchy that promotes organization and responsibility amongst its staff and management. Staff members and management alike must adhere to the corporate values this group holds: respect, kindness, and originality. Failure to acknowledge company values by demonstrating acts of disrespect and insubordination will result in punishment leading up to immediate termination.

1.5.        Removal of Board Members: Board members may be terminated from their position at the discretion of the Chief Executive Officer or the Chairmen. This may be for a variety of reasons such as inactivity, insubordination, failure to follow other rules & regulations, or infectivity in their given position.

1.6        Appointment of Board Members: Board members are appointed by the Chief Executive Officer or Chairmen. Board members are not required to have previous experience or affiliation with their given department or Robloxian Adventures however it is recommended.

1.7.            Ranking​: ​The Robloxian Adventures classifies several ranks that are considered to be management and leadership positions. Official ranks are defined in no particular order and are subjected to change without prior notice:

 

Ranks with ​*​indicate that they are on the Board of Directors by default​.

E. Director of Public Outreach*

1.8.            COO / RA Overseers​: ​Each Department has a Chief Operating Officer Overseer assigned to oversee the department. These members work with the Director of the department to ensure everything is running smoothly and to give input on the department. These users will be ranked RA Overseer in the respective discord server and in the roblox group and any other groups, they will be ranked Advisor. If you do not feel comfortable following the chain of command for any reason whatsoever, feel free to contact the respective overseer with the issue. In addition to this, the COO’s are given the task of assisting in RA as a whole to provide ideas and work on tasks to improve relations between community and staff. This position is assigned by the Chief Executive Officer or the Chairman of the Board and can be removed by those people. The Current Overseers and their respective departments are as followed:

  1. thejanator: Robloxian Adventures Training Academy

  1. Morinth20: Robloxian Adventures Search and Rescue

Article II: Rules and Regulations

 

2.1.            Respect​: ​Robloxian Adventures and its subsidiaries operate through holding our company values of respect, kindness, and originality. We expect all our staff to maintain these values and demonstrate them by adhering to Roblox’s terms and conditions. Failure to respect one another on the Robloxian Adventures discord, discords of official expeditions associated with Robloxian Adventures, and any games made by or associated with Robloxian Adventures will result in a punishment up to termination.

2.2.     Raiding:  Any type of raiding within RA is strictly prohibited. This includes using bots or using a group of people to cause problems or to destroy a server whether it be spamming words that break the rules by yourself or with a group, mass demoting people with a bot or by your own account, using a bot to destroy every server channel and anything else that can be classified as raiding. Breaking this rule will result in an automatic removal from all of RA and its subsidiaries and will not be appealable. In addition to this, an immediate blacklist will be issued if a mass demotion happens or if an RA server is destroyed.

 

2.3.            Racism​: ​Racism is prohibited. Under no circumstances, is racism allowed in Robloxian Adventures and its subsidiaries. Robloxian Adventures staff and management that are associated with the group and/or its institutions are subjected to punishment up to complete removal if racism is verified and demonstrated.

 

2.4.            Insubordination​:​ Insubordination is prohibited. Insubordination is defined as: ​the quality or condition of being insubordinate, or of being disobedient to authority; defiance​. Robloxian Adventures staff and management that are associated with the group and/or its institutions are subjected to punishment up to termination if insubordination is verified and demonstrated.

 

2.5.            Corruption and Bribery​: Any acts of corruption or bribery in Robloxian Adventures is prohibited. Unjustified promotions and firings, gaining personal monetary benefits, acts of dictatorship, and admin abuse will not be tolerated under any circumstances and all individuals who are proven to have conducted those violations will be subjected to immediate termination without appeal. 

2.6.            Inappropriateness and Harassment​: ​Robloxian Adventures is a child-friendly environment that adheres to ​Roblox terms and conditions​. The owners strive to keep this environment appropriate for all ages and take inappropriateness seriously. Inappropriate behavior and conversation subjects include but are not limited to: drugs, sex, gore, etc. is prohibited on Roblox and the Robloxian Adventures discord. Individuals regardless of rank or association with Robloxian Adventures that conduct these offenses or any other types of undefined acts of harassment are subjected to immediate termination without appeal.

2.6.1.   Prejudice, and hate rhetoric: Any forms of homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, along with racism will not be tolerated. RA can not control your opinion, however you can not use rhetoric within RA’s discords and affiliated discords that will show hate towards other people.

 

 

Acts of inappropriateness and harassment include but are not limited to:

a. Providing users’ personal information

b. Cyber bullying

c. Using or encouraging the use of excessive profanity (swearing)

d. Child endangerment

e. Hate speech or actions

f. Impersonation and defamation

g. Threats and self-harm

h. Looking for dates or partners.

i. Sexual content or overly violent content

j. Dangerous, unethical and illegal activities

 

2.6.            Asset Credibility and Ownership​: ​All assets of the Robloxian Adventures brand are considered and acknowledged to be under the ownership of Robloxian Adventures and Robloxian Adventures only. Unless stated otherwise in a contracted agreement by a third-party or permission from the founder, assets owned by the Robloxian Adventures are not to be shared on public databases or sold for monetary gain. If assets owned by Robloxian Adventures are copied, leaked, or sold without permission Robloxian Adventures reserves the right to escalate issues to the Roblox Developer Relations team.

 

2.7.           Discord​: ​The Robloxian Adventures official Discord operates in a manner that is independent of Roblox’s terms and conditions but follows the discord terms of service as well as other rules that are applied. However, the rules on the group Discord are located under #rules and may be altered and/or removed at any time without prior notice. These rules are implemented to maintain a safe and enjoyable environment for our staff and community members. ​Violations made on our Discord may contribute to a demotion or termination in our Roblox group​.

2.8.     Discord TOS: Robloxian Adventures follows the community guidelines and TOS and requires all participants and community members to adhere to those said rules. Failure to follow the Discord community guidelines will result in punishment depending on the severity of the rules and moderation action will be taken for any of these rules broken.

2.9.        Blacklist: A blacklist is defined as total and complete removal from all Robloxian Adventures assets including all Discords, Roblox groups and games. A blacklist can be issued in the case that an individual is seen to be too troublesome to be kept in the community. This can be for but not limited to: repeatedly causing issues, harassing members, raiding the group, severe threats, among more. To get fully RA blacklisted, a blacklist vote must be proposed by the Chairman, Chief Executive Officer or a Chief Operating Officer. The reasons for the blacklist along with any evidence will be presented if necessary. For a full RA blacklist to pass, a 3/4 board vote majority is needed. To get un-blacklisted from RA, the person in question must wait at least 31 days from their original blacklist to appeal. To appeal you must DM the Chairman, Chief Executive Officer, or Chief Operating Officer to set up an appeal and answer some questions that they’ll ask before hand. The next step is a board vote to start the appeal case. This requires a simple majority vote of more than 1/2. This is a vote to start the appeal case. Once the case is started an appeal channel will be started in which all eligible voters will have access. The individual appealing will get to thoroughly defend themselves and state their case. After this, the individual appealing will be removed and the board will discuss and vote. To fully un-blacklist someone, another vote of 3/4 must be reached in favor. It is up to the directors of each department if they’d rather an appeal or keep them banned from the respective department and will go through a strict probation.

 

 

 

 

Article III: Expeditions

 

3.1.            Official Expeditions​: ​Official expedition groups that are in the enemy’s section of the RA Roblox Group. They ​are classified as groups who have obtained the necessary requirements to be deemed official. Official expedition groups are eligible to have a base camp at Mount Everest for a starting fee of R$6,000. Taxes may be announced, failure to pay these fees will result in that expedition’s camp being removed from Everest. Fees are collected through the group store via. designated T-shirts or group payout. ​Issues that pertain to official expeditions can be addressed to the Chief Executive Officer​.

 

Official Expedition Information

 

Requirements expedition groups must have to be official:

 

  • The ROBLOX group must have at least 100 legitimate members.
  • Bots or alternate accounts to increase member count do not count.
  • The expedition group must contain a link to both the Robloxian Adventures  ROBLOX group and communication server in their ROBLOX expedition group and Discord server.
  • The discord server must have a minimum of 25 legitimate members.
  • Actively hosting expeditions in Mount Everest Roleplay and Annapurna.
  • The owner must not be banned or blacklisted from RA and its affiliated groups.
  • It is required for expedition members to be in the main RA Discord server.
  • Expeditions must submit a monthly report on the expedition’s status.
  • Expeditions must meet other requirements given by the Expedition Council.

 

If you have obtained all of the necessary requirements and are ready to become official please contact the Head of Expeditions through Discord or Roblox PM.

 

3.2.            Group Behavior​: ​Official expedition groups that are associated with Robloxian Adventures must understand they also represent the brand and must abide by Roblox terms and conditions as well as Robloxian Adventures rules.

 

Failure to abide by these rules will result in termination of the alliance and all assets associated with the group will be removed in-game without refund.

Article IV: Meetings

 

4.1.            Meeting Procedures​:​ Robloxian Adventures holds occasional meetings on a biweekly-monthly basis depending on accessibility and necessity  to promote professionalism, share ideas, and coordinate with each other. There are two types of meetings:

 

a. Board of Directors Meeting: A meeting with all of the directors & deputy directors, usually to discuss serious matters for the group as well as updates on the progress of each department.

 

b. Community Meeting: A meeting for the public to gather ideas and feedback from the community, as well as to announce major updates regarding games or the discord and news.

 

c. Staff/Board Meetings will be held anywhere from biweekly to monthly (subject to change). Staff meetings will be held on the 2nd Sunday of every Month, Board meetings on the last Sunday of every month. Community meetings will be held at the discretion of the Chief Executive Officer, Chairmen, or Chief Operating Officers.

 

d. Meetings are to be held on Discord unless noted otherwise.

e. Attendees are expected to act respectful and professional during any meeting and to follow the rules given out by the host.Failure to abide by this will result in being removed from the meeting and possible escalation depending on severity of the issue.

 

f. Meetings will typically be 15-30 minutes long. However, this is just an estimate as each meeting is different and will change depending on how much information needs to be shared.

 

g. Meeting notes will always be available for review after all meetings.

h. If a board member or a staff member cannot attend a mandatory meeting, they must inform the host ahead of time with reason as to why they’re unable to attend.

                                                             

           Article V: Administration

 

5.1.            Moderators​:​ Robloxian Adventures has a team of moderators that work around the clock to enforce the rules applied towards the group, its discord, and Roblox terms and conditions. They work directly under the supervision of Administrators and the Director of Moderation and Application Management. Official moderators are distinguished by the “Moderator” role on the Robloxian Adventures discord. Moderators reserve the right to enforce the rules through direct action that may include: warn, kick, mute, ban, etc. Under no circumstances are moderators allowed to violate the rules applied towards the group, discord, and Roblox terms and conditions. If a moderator violates any rules and proper evidence is provided, please report it to the Director of Moderation and Administration.

 

5.2.            Administrators​:​ Administrators are individuals who supervise and assist moderators by enforcing  rules applied towards the group, its discord, and Roblox terms and conditions. In serious rule violations, a moderator may request for assistance in a case in which the violation will be escalated to an administrator for further review. The Administration team is overseen by the Director of Moderation and Administration and the Deputy Director of Moderation and Administration.

5.3.        Lead Administrators: Lead administrators are the top ranking individuals in the moderation & administration department. These individuals are tasked with overseeing administrators and moderators and aid in the teaching of future moderators.

5.4.            Trial Moderators​: ​Trial Moderators are individuals who have just been recently hired as a moderator in their probationary phase. The probationary phase may include any additional training and observation by senior management.

5.5.        Warning, Kicking, & Banning: Each department may have their individual moderation guidelines. What is a constant is after three warnings you get kicked, after six total warnings you get banned. Certain circumstances warrant automatic bans such as raiding, spamming NSFW, toxicity, racial slurs, and offensive remarks/jokes. The Chief Executive Officer Chairman, Chief operating officer or Director of Moderation can ban members when necessary.

 

Moderator applications are announced typically through our job opportunity channel in the main RA discord. Trial Moderators are supervised by the Director of Moderation, Deputy Director and Lead Admins.

 

Article VI: Staff Guidelines

6.1.         Staff of Robloxian Adventures: Staff members of Robloxian Adventures are people who hold a current job within the departments that make up the community of Robloxian Adventures. This includes Robloxian Adventures, Public Outreach,Expedition council, Robloxian Adventures Training Academy, and Search and Rescue.

6.2.         Staff Behavior: Every staff member is expected to act mature and use grammar in their respective departments' public chat rooms. Acting Inappropriately (Defined in 2.6.) can result in termination depending on the severity. Staff rules are to be enforced by the Directors and Deputies of the departments.

6.3.         Staff Expectations: All staff members of Robloxian Adventures must adhere to the bylaws, main rules, and staff rules of Robloxian Adventures. Staff members must follow a chain of command which they can find in their respective departments. You may skip chain of command only if it's necessary (example would be not feeling comfortable talking to the director or deputy on a certain topic, you’d go to the respective COO.)

        

6.4.         Leaking of staff chats: Leaking is defined as releasing content that is not meant for the public viewing. This goes for all the departments including; Robloxian Adventures, Robloxian Adventures Training Academy, Public Outreach, and Search and Rescue. Leaking regular staff chats are prohibited unless approved by your Departments Director. Leaking of any Board Chats including group DMs are not allowed at all. If you are suspected of leaking, an investigation will take place and if found guilty, you are subjected to termination from your job without appeal. RA board chambers or associated chats (including group chats and appeal cases) are not to be leaked unless approved by The Chief Executive Officer or Chairman. 

 

 

 

Article VII: Development

 

1.1.            Developers​: ​Developers of Robloxian Adventures are tasked with updating and creating Robloxian Adventures games on a daily basis. Developers are often hired on a contracted basis and compensated on a pay-by-job system. The Robloxian Adventures development team is overseen directly by the Director of Development.

 

1.2.            Integrity​: Developers associated with Robloxian Adventures acknowledge and abide by the rules of Asset Credibility and Ownership (Article II Section 2.6). Violation of these rules will result in immediate termination and escalation to Roblox Developer Relations.

1.3        Work Ethic: Developers working for Robloxian Adventures are expected to work at a timely pace to ensure the success of Robloxian Adventures.

Article VIII: Summary

All members are required to follow the discord terms of service, robloxian adventures rules and roblox terms of service. Failing to adhere to any of the above information will result in warning, ban, blacklist or termination (only if you’re a part of staff.) We want RA to be a welcoming and fun community. If you have any questions regarding anything in the document above, please do not hesitate to contact a COO+ with your concerns and questions.
